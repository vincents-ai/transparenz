## How to Sign This CLA

By adding your information to the `cla-signatures.json` file and submitting a pull request, you indicate your acceptance of this CLA.

### Signature Format

Add an entry to `cla-signatures.json`:

```json
{
  "name": "Your Full Name",
  "email": "your.email@example.com",
  "github": "yourgithubusername",
  "date": "YYYY-MM-DD",
  "signature": "I agree to the Transparenz Contributor License Agreement"
}
```

### Electronic Signature

Your electronic signature in the form of a pull request adding your information to `cla-signatures.json` constitutes your agreement to the terms of this CLA.
